nicolas peter benedikt fehr is an artist and musician based in berlin and munich.

























born 1989 in frankfurt am main with the sun in pisces and scorpio ascending on the horizon, (nick)i grew up in connecticut, hamburg, and south hessia. after receiving my diploma in media arts and scenography at the university for arts and design karlsruhe in 2018, i sat down to write this text. that is what i am doing right now. so let's recapitulate:

during the last few years, i composed music for, sang, and performed in various solo and collaborative music, film, and theatre productions; i directed, produced, and acted in my own films; i wrote poems, drew drawings, grew one or two grey hair, dyed them dark blue, loved lovers, sold ice-cream, meditated, created some alter-egos, thought a lot about death, the current state of the world, and how i might best be of service to others. most recently, i have fallen in love with indian classical music, the tarot de marseille, hildegard von bingen, and ginger. alternatively, h e r e is an illustrated example of my most recent past. then scroll further down for the more formal part of this about page.
