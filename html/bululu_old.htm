Bululú

2017, Mixed-media installation, 4K-video, 6-channel audio, 45 min

Bululú is an overarching work that showcases the lives and works of Nicolas Fehr's alter-egos. It was presented as a filmic installation that spanned across the entire former women’s prison on Soehtstr. 7 in Berlin.
